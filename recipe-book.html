Recipe Book

A concept mobile app for exploring, sharing recipes and connecting with food lovers

Read Case Study

Overview

I tried my hands on designing a concept app. The concept was to have a mobile where people can connect search recipes and also share recipes. A colleague initially contacted me to design a recipe app for a project, I did a few screens and decided in the end to share the source file as a design freebie on uplabs.

The freebie was featured as the Best of the Day in Android on Uplabs with over 3,000 views!

ROLE IN PROJECT

User Experience Designer

DURATION

1 Week

The Challenge

1. Two mobile applications were planned to be designed in a short time frame, one for students and another for lecturers. I had to work on designing for both user types simultaneously.

Visual Elements

The goal was to come up with a clean minimal design with a delightful feel. I went the route of using cards to group and visually represent information
